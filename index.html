Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • News feed not refreshing? Please check your internet connection and try pulling down on the screen to refresh the latest articles.
  • Want to manage notifications? You can easily enable or disable push notifications from the "Settings" tab within the app.
  • Weather widget issues? Ensure that Location Services are enabled for the app. Go to Settings > Daily Scope > Location and select "While Using the App or Widgets".
